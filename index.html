Installing the Crunchyroll Party Extension: A Step-by-Step Guide

Imagine the possibilities! Crunchyroll Watch Party is your perfect solution if you love binge-watching anime with loved ones, but distance keeps you apart. Say goodbye to boredom and hello to endless fun as you stream anime, manga, and dorama with your friends using this incredible extension. Therefore, here’s a quick and easy guide to install and start using the Crunchyroll Party Chrome extension:

  • Download Crunchyroll Party Extension You need to install the Crunchyroll extension on your device to get started. Then, click here to install the Crunchyroll extension for free, or visit the official Chrome Web Store and search for the Crunchyroll extension. Remember, you will need either the Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome browser to install and use Crunchyroll Watch Party. The extension is compatible with Windows, macOS, Chromebooks, PCs, and laptops. Further, enjoy seamless anime streaming with your friends, no matter where you are!
  • Pin the Extension to Your Toolbar After installation, pin the extension icon to your toolbar for easy access later. If you don't see the icon? Then, click on the puzzle piece icon in the top right corner of your browser. Then, locate the Crunchyroll extension in the list and pin it to your toolbar for quick and convenient use.
  • Visit Crunchyroll With the Crunchyroll Party extension installed and pinned, head over to the Crunchyroll website and log in to your account. Moreover, ensure you have your own Crunchyroll account, as it’s required to host or join a Crunchyroll Party. Get ready to enjoy anime with friends, no matter where they are!
  • Start Your Favorite Anime or Any Video Next, search for the anime you want to enjoy with your friends at your Crunchyroll Watch Party, and hit play. You can watch any anime, manga, dorama, or other series and movies with anyone worldwide. Further, get ready for a shared viewing experience like no other!
  • Host A Crunchyroll Party It’s party time! Click on the extension icon you pinned to your toolbar earlier. A small "CREATE A WATCH PARTY" window will appear with a “START A PARTY” button. Then, click the button, and you’ll receive an invitation link to share with friends for your Crunchyroll Party.
  • Share the Invitation Link Now that you have your Crunchyroll Party invitation link, it's time to gather your friends. Share the link with everyone you want to join your watch party. Whether through social media, messaging apps, or email, ensure your friends get the invite and are ready to enjoy anime together in perfect sync. The more, the merrier!
  • Join a Crunchyroll Party Want to join a Crunchyroll Party and watch your favorite anime with friends? Here's how: first, download and pin the extension. Next, log into your Crunchyroll account. Then, click on the invitation link you received. That's all it takes! You'll instantly be part of an amazing Crunchyroll Watch Party, ready to enjoy anime with your friends.
